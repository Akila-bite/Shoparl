About Me

Hi! My name is Amanda, I am an artist and YouTuber based in Toronto, Canada. On my social media platforms, I share content about art and journaling to inspire others to bring more creativity into their daily lives. I started Shop AmandaRachLee to provide distinctive, premium stationery items that I personally design. I have been getting requests from my audience to launch a stationery line for a while now. Reading the thousands of comments saying this made me feel supported yet absolutely terrified. It was a lot of pressure. I did not want to sell anything that I would not use or value for myself. Because of this, it took me years bring my full vision to life. But, Shop AmandaRachLee is FINALLY here and it's all inspired by you. I created every product with you guys (my lil doodles) in mind - between taking your suggestions and trying to come up with solutions for the common journaling & doodling problems that you guys tell me about. I'm so proud of these products. I hope they add a daily dose of creativity, productivity and mindfulness to your life. This is just the beginning. Thank you for everything, and as always, keep doodling!
